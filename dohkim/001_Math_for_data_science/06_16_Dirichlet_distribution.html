\dfrac{1}{B(\alpha_1,\alpha_2, \cdots ,\alpha_K)} (x_1^{\alpha_1-1} \times x_2^{\alpha_2-1} \times \cdots \times x_K^{\alpha_K-1})\\ = \dfrac{1}{B(\alpha_1,\alpha_2, \cdots ,\alpha_K)} \prod\limits_{i=1}^K x_i^{\alpha_i-1}$$$ $$$Dir()$$$: PDF of Dirichlet distribution $$$x_1, \cdots ,x_K$$$: values of Dirichlet distribution $$$\alpha_1,\alpha_2, \cdots ,\alpha_K$$$: parameters of Dirichlet distribution $$$B(\alpha_1,\alpha_2, \cdots ,\alpha_K)$$$: Beta function $$$B(\alpha_1,\alpha_2, \cdots ,\alpha_K) = \dfrac{\Gamma(\alpha_1)\times \Gamma(\alpha_2) \times \cdots \times \Gamma(\alpha_K)} {\Gamma(\alpha_1+\alpha_2+ \cdots +\alpha_K)}$$$ ================================================== Relationship between Beta distribution and Dirichlet distribution Beta
